02 · How it works

Here's what ships.

The files and skills that configure Claude around your business.

Company layer · written once during build
├── what-is-[company].mdcompany identity
├── how-we-work.mdteam structure, processes
└── how-we-communicate.mdbrand voice
Individual layer · created by /onboard, one per person
└── Individual Profiles/
└── [name] - Claude Profilewho-i-am + how-i-talk + how-i-work
Engagement layer · created by /new-project, one per client
└── [client]/
├── project-brief.mdscope, contacts, timeline
└── project-log.mdrunning log, updated by every skill
Specialist layer · 10 named experts that read the layers above
├── /client-comms
├── /content-strategist
├── /project-manager
├── /brand-strategist
└── + 6 more
Workflow layer · 4 standing routines, on cue or on schedule
├── /prep-for-callpre-meeting brief
├── /weekly-reviewfriday consolidation
├── /daily-logend-of-day capture, scheduled
└── /refine-voicevoice profile updates from real evidence

Every Friday the system reviews its own logs and proposes what to tighten. You approve. The system upgrades itself.

The Foundation is where every engagement starts. From there: automation builds for the workflows the install doesn't cover, and full operating systems for teams that want every function running on context.

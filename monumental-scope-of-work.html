Scope of work

Claude System Foundation

Your team is already using Claude. This engagement builds the shared system around it — so everyone works from the same context, the same standards, and the same voice.

What we're solving

Right now, your team's Claude usage is individual and isolated. Everyone has their own conversations, their own context, and their own approach. The result is inconsistency — good output sometimes, generic output other times, and no reliable way to hand work from one person to the next without manually restoring context each time.

The Foundation fixes that at the system level. Not by changing how your team works, but by giving Claude a shared understanding of how your business works — so it stops being a tool people prompt and starts being something they delegate to.

What's included

Company context files

I work with you to write a set of files that teach Claude who Monumental is — your brand voice, your brand standards, how your team works, and how you communicate with clients. This lives in a shared Drive folder your whole team can access and maintain.

Guided onboarding flow for each team member

A plugin-based flow that walks each person through setting up their own personal context — their role, voice, and preferences. Runs once per person, takes about ten minutes, and connects their Claude workspace to the shared Drive. After that, Claude knows who they are every time they open a new project.

New-project setup flow

Every time a team member starts a new client engagement project in Claude, a two-minute flow loads your company context, their personal profile, and asks a few questions about the specific project. Claude starts each project already knowing your business — no re-explaining, no starting from scratch.

Ten specialist roles

Ten pre-built specialist roles — project manager, client comms specialist, content strategist, data analyst, and more. Each one knows Monumental's context, so the output is ready to use, not ready to rewrite.

Delegation guide

A plain-language document for your team: what to hand off to Claude, how to brief it, and what good output looks like. Includes a quick-start orientation so anyone new to the system can get up to speed in twenty minutes.

60-minute training session (recorded)

I walk your team through the system live — how to use it, how to delegate, and how to get the most out of every project. The recording becomes your team's reference for anyone who joins later.

30-day launch support

I stay available as the team settles in — questions answered, adjustments made as needed. Around week two we'll talk about what to automate first. The Foundation is the starting point, not the finish line.

What's not included

The Foundation builds the shared system and trains your team to use it. A few things fall outside that scope and would be quoted separately:

  • Additional custom skills beyond what's included ($150/hr each)
  • Custom integrations with proprietary tools not available in Claude's connector marketplace
  • Ongoing updates after the 30-day window — available as a Partner retainer
